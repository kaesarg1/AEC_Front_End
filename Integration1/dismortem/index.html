Ego. Deo Optimo Maximo

Bio

We are the witness of the individual's decadence, fueled by the post-truth stupidity, metahistorical repetition of bias, and the worst of all: non-endless superstition and trickery, coming from the cults ignorance. No left or right. No above or below. No right or wrong. Just individuals in the middle of this life of paradise, on behalf of a living hell. Dissonance and death. Dismortem.
